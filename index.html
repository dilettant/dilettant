Welcome to dilettant

We believe that shared knowledge
can enhance people powered change

We initiate research projects addressing organizational, cooperative and political strategies aimed at a broad social context.

The aim of our projects is to find ways of sharing and facilitating the knowledge, thinking and support structures that cooperative practices can produce, as well as ways of sharing costs and risks.

We also works as writers, consultants and coaches for artists, public agencies and institutions, educational programs, interest groups and organizations, and engage in local cultural political processes.
